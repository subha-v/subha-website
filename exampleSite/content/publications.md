## HILITE: Human-in-the-loop Interactive Tool for Image Editing
**Vadlamannati, S.**, Pasumarthi, A, Yang, D., Neubig, G., Khanuja, S., et al.

*Proceedings of the 2025 Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing (EMNLP) - HCI + NLP Workshop*

An open-source interactive image-editing platform that integrates six state-of-the-art diffusion models to generalize across diverse editing tasks, including style transfer and object swapping.

## Partial Tensorized Transformers for Natural Language Processing
**Vadlamannati, S.** and Solgi, R.

*Proceedings of the 16th International Conference on Agents and Artificial Intelligence (ICAART 2023)*

A novel approach to compress vision-language models using partially tensorized neural networks, achieving 53% compression while improving accuracy by up to 5%.

## Metric-Based In-context Learning: A Case Study in Text Simplification
**Vadlamannati, S.** and Gozde, S.

*Proceedings of the 16th International Natural Language Generation Conference (INLG 2023)*

**Nominated for Best Paper**
